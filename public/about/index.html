What makes a Central Coast Tree Services job different.

One person, start to finish.

Scott quotes the job and runs the crew that does the job. There is no handover, no junior estimator who lowballs the quote then upcharges on the day, and no third-party project manager who hasn't been on a job site in five years. Every quote we issue is all-inclusive — site assessment, weather risk, access risk, permit work if relevant. No "from $X" range pricing, no day-of-build surprise variations, no mid-job upsell. If a surprise lands on the dig that we did not allow for, it's our cost, not yours.
